# Cross-Selling-Dashboard
This Power BI dashboard identifies existing motor vehicle and travel insurance policyholders and highlights potential opportunities to cross-sell health insurance policies. It visualizes customer segments, policy overlap, and target groups to help sales teams focus on promoting health policies to eligible clients, increasing cross-selling effectiveness and revenue.

Power BI dashboard targeting motor and travel policyholders for health policy cross-selling. Uses Python scripts and predictive analysis to identify potential customers, enabling focused outreach to boost health policy adoption and increase sales.
